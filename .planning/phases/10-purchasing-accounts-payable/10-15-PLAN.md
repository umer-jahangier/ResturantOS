<objective>
Make the purchasing analytics page usable across periods and vendors.

Purpose: UAT tests 10, 14 and 15 all came back "partial". The page renders, but `/app/purchasing/analytics/page.tsx` hardcodes the period to the current month (no picker) and pins `VendorScorecardCard` to `vendors[0]` (no selector). ROADMAP SC#5 asks for a scorecard "per vendor" and spend analytics "with period comparison" — a report you cannot point at a vendor or a period only accidentally satisfies that. The backend already supports both: `GET /analytics/spend?branchId&from&to` and `GET /analytics/scorecard?vendorId&branchId` (and `useSpendAnalytics` / `useVendorScorecard` already take those params). This is purely the missing controls.

Output: a period picker and a vendor selector wired into the hooks that already accept them.
</objective>
