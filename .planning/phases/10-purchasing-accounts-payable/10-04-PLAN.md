<design_decisions priority="high">
These are DECIDED â€” implement them as written, do not re-litigate:
- **Allowed source states â†’ CLOSED:** `FULLY_RECEIVED` (normal close, reason optional) and
  `PARTIALLY_RECEIVED` (short-close, reason MANDATORY). Every other state throws
  `InvalidPoStateException`, including closing an already-CLOSED PO (no silent idempotent no-op).
- **OPA gate:** short-close ONLY. Normal close of a FULLY_RECEIVED PO is routine bookkeeping already
  gated by `FEATURE_VENDOR` + existing security. Short-closing a PARTIALLY_RECEIVED PO writes off
  goods that were ordered but never received â€” that IS financially consequential, so it goes through
  OPA with action `vendor.po.close`. Denial â†’ `ApprovalLimitExceededException`.
- **Missing reason on short-close:** `InvalidPoStateException("Short-close requires a reason")` â€”
  mirrors how `PoApprovalService.reject()` demands a reason.
- **Event:** publish `PO_CLOSED` (topic `purchasing.topic`, routing key `purchasing.po.closed`) with
  payload {poId, vendorId, branchId, totalPaisa, closedBy, shortClosed (boolean), reason}. This is a
  NEW event, additive â€” no consumer required in Phase 10.
- **No finance posting on close.** Close is a workflow terminal state, not an accounting event. GR/IR
  was already posted on receipt and AP on invoice match. Do NOT auto-post a JE here.
- **Out of scope (document, don't build):** cancelling a SENT PO with zero receipts, and reopening a
  CLOSED PO. Both throw `InvalidPoStateException` today and stay that way.
</design_decisions>
