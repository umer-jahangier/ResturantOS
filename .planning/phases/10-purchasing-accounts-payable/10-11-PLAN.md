<objective>
Make Purchasing reachable. Fix the phantom `FEATURE_PURCHASING` flag, stop nav-flag drift from ever recurring, and turn the purchasing shell into something you can navigate.

Purpose: this is the blocker that made the whole module invisible. `frontend/components/shared/sidebar-nav-items.ts` (lines ~77 and ~182) gates Purchasing on `FEATURE_PURCHASING` ‚Äî a flag that exists nowhere in the backend. The real flag is `FEATURE_VENDOR` (`TierFeatureDefaults`, `RouteFeatureMap` `/api/v1/purchasing/ -> FEATURE_VENDOR`, every `@RequiresFeature`). `FeatureGuard` fails OPEN only on fetch error, not on an absent flag ‚Äî so whenever flags load *successfully*, the nav item never renders and the entire module is reachable only by typing a URL. On top of that, `/app/purchasing` unconditionally `redirect()`s to `/vendors` and `PurchasingTabs` only lists Vendors + Analytics, so even a URL-typing user can't get anywhere else.

Output: correct flag, a typed canonical flag set with a drift test, a real landing page, and a full tab bar.
</objective>
