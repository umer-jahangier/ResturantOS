<objective>
Create the `finance.period.open` permission and grant it to exactly the three finance-capable roles, via a new Liquibase changeset — the RBAC prerequisite that gates the self-service provision endpoint (plan 05). This must land in wave 1 so the permission exists in the auth DB before the phase's live end-to-end verification (plan 06).

Purpose: Establish least-privilege authorization for `POST /api/v1/finance/periods/provision`. Because changeset `036`'s wildcard `SELECT ... FROM permissions` grants to OWNER/TENANT_ADMIN are `runOnChange="false"` and only ran once at initial DB creation, a brand-new permission code will NOT retroactively reach OWNER/TENANT_ADMIN — this changeset must grant all three roles explicitly.
Output: Changeset `044-finance-period-open-permission.xml`, its registration in the master changelog, and a DB-assertion IT proving the grants (and the deny for other roles).
</objective>
