<objective>
Deliver stock counts with variance posting plus low-stock and expiry alerts (INV-06): a count-sheet post that computes variance against system quantity and adjusts stock, a low-stock alert on reorder-point breach, and a single nightly `@Scheduled` FEFO expiry sweep that publishes `EXPIRY_ALERT` for lots nearing expiry.

Purpose: Perpetual inventory needs periodic recounts to correct drift, and operators need proactive low-stock and shelf-life warnings — implemented as enterprise-standard scheduled sweeps, not per-batch timers.
Output: Stock-count model + variance service, the low-stock alert path, and the nightly expiry sweep, each with integration proof (the sweep tested by direct invocation with a fixed clock).
</objective>
