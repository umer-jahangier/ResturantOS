<objective>
Build the new `kitchen-service` (port 8090, `kitchen_db`) Kitchen Display System: consume `ORDER_SENT_TO_KDS`, route items to per-station tickets, drive the PENDING->COOKING->READY lifecycle, publish `ORDER_READY` (consumed back by POS), and serve the always-dark WebSocket KDS board. Critically, implement the dedicated **kitchen-only role** with strict access isolation per the user's requirement: a kitchen login can do ONLY kitchen operations, while cashier/finance users are completely blocked from the kitchen, and the owner sees everything.

Purpose: closes the POS->kitchen loop (KDS-01/02) and delivers the requested security separation: distinct kitchen identity, least-privilege, fail-closed OPA, both at the API/WebSocket and the UI.
Output: kitchen-service with routing/lifecycle/WebSocket, the POS `ORDER_READY` consumer, the `KITCHEN_STAFF` role + `pos.kds.*` permissions + isolation tests/policies, and the dark KDS board UI.
</objective>
