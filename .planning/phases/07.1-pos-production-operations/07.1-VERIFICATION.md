### Gaps Summary

Two genuine, code-level gaps were found — both real, both distinct from the disclosed "known debt" list (RabbitMQ IT blocker, finance-period-locked S5 gate, duplicate exception handler):

1. **BLOCKER — CHARGE NOW can become unclickable after Send-to-Kitchen.** Live-reproduced: the settlement footer (CHARGE NOW / Void) sits in the same bottom-right screen region as the global toast notifications. A "Rev N sent to kitchen" success toast fired right before a charge attempt visually/functionally blocks the CHARGE NOW button; Sonner's hover-pauses-dismiss behavior means the toast can be kept alive indefinitely by the very act of moving the pointer toward the button. This breaks the realistic "send new items, then charge" cashier flow and is a direct failure of Roadmap SC-7 / POS-14's "a cashier can charge... through the UI" acceptance.
2. **WARNING — Order-level "Kitchen Notes" never reach the KDS board.** Per-item notes work; the order-level special-instructions callout has no backend data path (disclosed in prior SUMMARYs, but still a live, unfixed functional gap against Roadmap SC-5/SC-6 and POS-13/KDS-03).

Everything else — the item-status/derived-status model, order revisions (including a genuine V4-migration omission the phase itself found and fixed via V5), table-centric dine-in, Order Management, the KDS stable-sort/per-item-status/revision-pill redesign, till/void/offline-sync, menu search, and the first-item/item-cap terminal bug fixes — is solidly implemented, unit-tested (160/160 frontend, full pos-service/kitchen-service unit suites green), and live-E2E-confirmed working (S1-S4 of the settlement flow passed cleanly in this session's own fresh run).
