## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Severity | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-08.2-171 | Elevation of Privilege | Stock write dialogs reachable by a view-only role | high | mitigate | All four triggers are wrapped in `PermissionGuard require="inventory.item.manage"`, and each endpoint authorizes independently server-side. |
| T-08.2-172 | Information Disclosure | Reading another branch's on-hand stock | high | mitigate | `useStockLevels` passes the current branch, and the endpoint rejects a branch id that differs from the caller's JWT claim (plan 08.2-02). |
| T-08.2-173 | Repudiation | Client-side thresholds disagreeing with server semantics, hiding a stock-out | medium | mitigate | Row emphasis reads the server-computed `belowReorderPoint` and `nonPositive` flags; no browser-side comparison against `reorderPoint` is permitted. |
| T-08.2-174 | Tampering | A mistyped ingredient identifier posting a movement against the wrong item | high | mitigate | Every ingredient selection goes through `CatalogItemCombobox`; no free-text identifier input exists in any dialog. |
| T-08.2-175 | Repudiation | Colour-only risk signalling failing colour-blind users | medium | mitigate | Every washed row also renders a status chip with an icon and a text label. |
