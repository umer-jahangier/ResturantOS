<objective>
Redesign the KDS into station-isolated New/Started/Preparing/Ready item-status columns with slim
cards and a dedicated detail page (KDS-04/D-12), and replace the aggressive aging treatment with
subtle prioritization driven by the station escalation threshold plus a single shared clock
(KDS-05/D-13). Consumes the 07.3-05 backend (item-status endpoint, table number, station seeding).

Purpose: Today the board renders stations-as-columns with a heavy card and a `Dialog` detail
(RESEARCH KDS-04), aging uses `animate-bounce`/full-red and a per-card `setInterval` that won't scale
(RESEARCH KDS-05), and the board is empty because no stations were seeded (fixed backend-side in
07.3-05).

Output: the new routes + station board + item columns + slim card + detail page, a shared clock, the
item-status mutation wiring, and the table-number field.
</objective>
