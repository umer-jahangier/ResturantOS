<objective>
Decouple payment recording from order close (POS-23) and expose a payments-history read path
(POS-22 backend). Recording a payment persists an `OrderPayment` and derives a payment status,
but does NOT close the order; an order transitions to `CLOSED` only through a single
`maybeCloseOrder` seam that fires from BOTH the payment flow and the mark-served flow, and only
when the order is fully Paid AND fully Served (`derivedStatus == SERVED`).

Purpose: The Phase-7.1 `closeOrder` recorded payment and flipped to CLOSED atomically, and never
persisted `OrderPayment`. This plan makes payment durable, makes close a derived consequence of
Paid+Served (D-08), and is the backend foundation every settlement UI plan (07, 08) depends on.

Output: `PaymentStatus` enum, `PaymentStatusDerivationService`, a `maybeCloseOrder` seam, a
`GET /orders/{id}/payments` endpoint, and the ITs proving the 4-cell (paid × served) close matrix.
</objective>
